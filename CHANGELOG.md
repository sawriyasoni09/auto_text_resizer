## [1.0.0] - 2025-08-01
### Added
- Initial release of `auto_text_resizer`.
- Auto-resizing `Text` widget that automatically adjusts font size to fit its container.
- Basic customization options:
    - `minFontSize`
    - `maxFontSize`
    - `stepGranularity`
    - `style`
- Example app included in `/example` to demonstrate usage.
